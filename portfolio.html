  • Drawing Performance
    • Brief
      • At the start of this project, we were given a household tool at random. We proceeded to draft this tool as an object, following which we drafted it's motion. This lead to the creation of several section cuts based on our motion drawings, extruding them into space, and ultimately merging ideas with a partner. The product was a wood model, created with a particular emphasis on tectonics and joinery. We used ash and popular, with virtually zero glue.

        The final project was purchased by the Dean of the College of Fine Arts, and is now on display in his office.
